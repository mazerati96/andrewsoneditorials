My Story

A lifelong reader who decided to make it official.

I’m Andrew, an editor with a long-standing passion for books, storytelling, and the craft of writing. Over the years, I’ve worked closely with writers at different stages of their journeys, helping them shape their ideas, strengthen their voices, and bring their work closer to publication.

I’m particularly interested in thoughtful, well-crafted fantasy, romance, horror, fiction and it sub-genres, and I value collaboration, clarity, and honesty in my editorial process. Whether working on early drafts or near-final manuscripts, my aim is always to support writers in making their work the best it can be.

When I’m not editing, you’ll usually find me reading widely, following new voices, and staying engaged with the conversations that shape contemporary writing. For more inquiries, Please fill out the form on the hire me page.

How I Work

The three things I bring to every manuscript

🎯

Honesty

Kind feedback isn't the same as useful feedback. I tell you what's working and what isn't — always constructively, never harshly.

🔬

Thoroughness

I read as both a reader and an editor — catching what pulls me out of the story and why, down to the sentence level.

💡

Collaboration

It's your story, your voice, your vision. My job is to help you realise it fully — not to rewrite it in my image.
