About

Passionate about stories — and the people who tell them.

Hi! I'm Andrew Agada, the reader and editor behind Andrewson Editorials. I specialize in beta reading, editing, and I bring a deep love of storytelling to every manuscript I touch.

I've worked with writers at every stage — from messy first drafts to near-final manuscripts — helping them see their work with fresh eyes and leave more confident in their craft. My approach is thorough, encouraging, and always honest.

What I Offer

Services built around your manuscript

Every project is different. Here's how I can help.

📖

Beta Reading

A reader's-eye view of your full manuscript — pacing, plot holes, character consistency, emotional resonance, and everything in between.

✏️

Developmental Editing

Big-picture feedback on structure, character arcs, theme, and narrative flow to strengthen the foundation of your story.

🔍

Line Editing

Sentence-level polish — clarity, voice, style, and flow — so your prose reads exactly the way it should.

✂️

Copy Editing

Grammar, punctuation, consistency, and syntax — the technical layer that makes your manuscript clean and credible.

🧐

Proofreading

The final pass before publication — catching any remaining typos, errors, or formatting slip-ups that slipped through.

📐

Formatting

Manuscript formatting to industry standards — ready for submission to agents, publishers, or self-publishing platforms.
